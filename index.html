Play Snake

Instructions

Controls:
   Movement: up, down, left, right or w, a, s, d keys
   Restart Game: p key

Rules:
   1. Eat apples to increase your score (red circles).
   2. Don't hit rocks (grey circles).
   3. Don't eat your own tail!

Press the up, left, or right arrow keys (or w,a,d keys) to start the game and move your snake (green object) in that direction.

Also, your Snake will gradually increase in size, good luck!
